Welcome to Half-Brewed, the Potion Store

Magic for every taste — from classics to curiosities

About Us

At Half-Brewed, we're passionate about potions — from the humble Healing Brew to the mysterious Sparkle Surprise™.

Tucked away between a mossy boulder and a wandering trader, our little stand has been serving adventurers and misfits since the early days of the Overworld.

We're not a mega-factory. We're not a wizard tower. We're a cozy cauldron in the corner of chaos — and we like it that way.

What We Offer

We craft a wide variety of potions to fit your needs, moods, and questionable decisions.

  • Combat-ready brews — Strength, Speed, Regeneration
  • Exploration essentials — Night Vision, Water Breathing, Fire Resistance
  • Social experiments — Invisibility, Jump Boost, Slippery Talk (don`t ask)

How We Brew

All our potions are brewed in small batches using locally-sourced ingredients — from creeper dust to fermented spider eye (we don't judge).

We mix tradition with experimentation. Some recipes are ancient. Others were discovered by accident during lightning storms. Either way, we believe in the magic of making something weird and wonderful.

Who We Serve

  • A villager hoping to impress your crush
  • A curious traveler with a few emeralds to spare

there's something for everyone at Half-Brewed.
